Part 95: GMRS, FRS, MURS, and CB radio

GMRS & FRS Radio

GMRS (General Mobile Radio Service) and FRS (Family Radio Service) are pretty much the same. Except GMRS requires a license that costs $75 nad no test. FRS requires no license but your power is very limited... GMRS You can do at most 50 Watts on CERTAIN frequencies FRS is mostly 2 watts. GMRS you also permited to use a non-linked repeater that is not linked to the telephone network.
