Telecommunications United States Code Title 47

About

Title 47 is the Telecommunications book that goes over certain rules whether it be digital or radio. This is mostly regulated by the FCC (Federal Communications Commission)

This website will not go over every section of Title 47, it is way to long. Also this website is not supported or endorsed by the FCC

Commonly referenced parts:

Part 15: Concerning unlicensed broadcasts and spurious emissions

Part 73: Radio Broadcast Services.

Part 95: Concerning GMRS, FRS, MURS, and CB radio

Part 97: Concerning amateur radio
