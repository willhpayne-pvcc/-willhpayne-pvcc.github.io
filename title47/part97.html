Part 97: Amateur Radio

What is Amateur Radio??

According to the FCC, Amateur Radio is the art of radio and noncommercial communications, particularly with respect to providing emergency communications. However, the service can be used in a number of ways such as just rag chew (normal conversations)

Can I use Amateur Radio and set up station now??

Short answer here is no.... However, you can use the service if you pass an Amateur Radio Exam with a 74% (on all test levels) or better and pay the FCC $35

If you fail to get a license with a call sign like KQ4NTQ and you are caught transmitting without such license you will be fined $10,000 (unless it is an emergency).

First, you must start with a Technician Class here are the license levels:

  1. Technician, 35 Questions
  2. General, 35 Questions
  3. Extra, 50 Questions
